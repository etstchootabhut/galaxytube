3 Idiots

3 Idiots is a 2009 Indian Hindi-language coming-of-age comedy-drama film written, edited, and directed by Rajkumar Hirani. The film is loosely adapted from Chetan Bhagat's novel "Five Point Someone" and produced by Vidhu Vinod Chopra under the banner of Vinod Chopra Films. Starring Aamir Khan, R. Madhavan, and Sharman Joshi in the titular roles, the story follows the friendship of three engineering students at an Indian college and satirizes the Indian education system.
