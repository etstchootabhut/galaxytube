Bahubali: The Beginning

Baahubali: The Beginning is a 2015 Indian epic fantasy action film directed by S.S. Rajamouli and produced by Shobu Yarlagadda and Prasad Devineni under Arka Media Works. The film, shot in Telugu and Tamil, follows Shivudu, an adventurous young man who helps his love Avantika rescue Devasena, the former queen of Mahishmati, from the tyrannical rule of king Bhallaladeva.
