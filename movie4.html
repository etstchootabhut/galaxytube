Avengers Endgame

Avengers: Endgame revolves around the surviving members of the Avengers and their allies attempting to reverse the catastrophic events caused by Thanos in Infinity War, who erased half of all life in the universe with the Infinity Stones. The film explores the emotional and physical toll of this event on the characters, as they grapple with loss and the need to restore the universe. The Avengers devise a plan to travel back in time to retrieve the Infinity Stones before Thanos can use them, leading to a series of complex and emotionally charged interactions with past versions of themselves and other characters.
